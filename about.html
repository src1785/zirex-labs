Zirex: India's Exclusive Zirconia Dental Lab

Welcome to Zirex, the CAD/CAM Zirconia dental lab dedicated solely to high-performance restorative solutions. We deliver digital precision, guaranteed reliability, and unmatched clinical support for every case.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate compromise. We provide clinicians with 100% proof-tested Zirconia restorations designed for long-term clinical success, ensuring maximum reliability and minimal chair time

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier Zirconia-exclusive laboratory partner. We set the benchmark for restoration strength (outperforming Grade 4 Titanium) and consistent aesthetics across all full-contour Zirconia and layered cases.

Precision Through Digital Workflow

Our processes are defined by technology, ensuring every restoration benefits from perfect scan-to-mill accuracy and quality control.

1

Digital Intake

Accepting all major intraoral scanner files and utilizing CAD software for precise design and fit.

2

Milling & Sintering

High-speed, five-axis milling of Y-TZP Zirconia followed by programmed sintering for peak density and strength.

3

Quality Check

Every single unit undergoes a mechanical stress test (the equivalent of a 100% proof test) before final finishing.
